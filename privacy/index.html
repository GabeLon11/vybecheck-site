Privacy Policy

Last updated: January __, 2026

VybeCheck helps people share venue “vybes.” This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Profile info: username, display name, optional bio/avatar.
  • Location info: approximate location used to show nearby venues and enable location-based features.
  • Posts: your mood tag, emoji, crowd score, comment, and the venue you posted at.
  • Device/usage: basic diagnostics to keep the app reliable (crash/bug context).

How we use information

  • To run core features (nearby venues, posting, feeds, favorites, badges).
  • To keep the community safe (moderation, abuse prevention, reports).
  • To improve the product (debugging, performance, feature planning).

Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (hosting, databases, email tools) strictly to operate VybeCheck.

Your choices

  • You can disable location in iOS Settings (some features may not work).
  • You can request deletion of your data (see Support page).

Contact

Questions? Contact us at admin@vybecheckapp.com.
