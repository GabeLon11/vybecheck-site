Community Guidelines

Last updated: January __, 2026

VybeCheck is about sharing the vibe, not tearing people down.

Not allowed

  • Harassment, hate, threats, or targeted abuse
  • Sexual content involving minors, or exploitation
  • Doxxing or sharing private personal info
  • Illegal activity, violence, or self-harm encouragement
  • Spam, scams, or impersonation

Reporting

Use the in-app Report button on posts. We review reports and may remove content or restrict accounts.

Be real

  • Keep it about the venue vibe
  • Don’t post private info about others
  • Respect staff and other guests

These guidelines may evolve as the community grows.
