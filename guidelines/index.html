Community Guidelines

VybeCheck is for sharing what a place feels like — keep it respectful and safe.

Not allowed

  • Harassment, hate, threats, or targeted abuse.
  • Doxxing or sharing personal information about others.
  • Sexual content involving minors (instant ban + report).
  • Illegal content or encouraging illegal activity.
  • Spam or impersonation.

Reporting

You can report posts in-app. We review reports and may remove content or restrict accounts.

Contact

If something urgent comes up, email admin@vybecheckapp.com.
